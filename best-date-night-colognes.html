How to Apply Cologne for a Date

Less is more. Two to three sprays is all you need. One on the neck, one on the wrist, and optionally one on the chest. You want your date to notice your scent when they lean in, not when they walk into the room.

Apply to pulse points. Your neck, wrists, and chest generate heat, which helps the fragrance project naturally throughout the evening.

Don't rub your wrists together. This breaks down the fragrance molecules and changes the scent. Just spray and let it dry naturally.

Moisturize first. Apply unscented lotion to your skin before spraying. Hydrated skin holds fragrance significantly longer — meaning your decant lasts more sprays too.
