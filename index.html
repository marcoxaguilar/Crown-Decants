What Is a Fragrance Decant?

A decant is a smaller portion of a full-sized cologne or perfume, transferred from the original bottle into a travel-friendly glass atomizer. It's the smartest way to try luxury fragrances without the luxury price tag.

Every decant from Crown Decants is hand-poured from a sealed, authentic retail bottle. No imitations. No "inspired by." Just the real thing.

About Crown Decants

We started Crown Decants because we believe everyone deserves to smell incredible — without spending $200+ on a bottle they might not even love.

We're fragrance obsessives who got tired of blind-buying full bottles only to let them collect dust on the shelf. So we built the store we wished existed: authentic, hand-poured decants from the best designer and niche fragrances in the game, at prices that make it easy to explore.

Every single decant is poured from a genuine, sealed bottle into a premium glass atomizer. We don't do knockoffs. We don't do "inspired by." If it says Creed on the label, it's Creed in the bottle. Period.
